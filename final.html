Government Cybersecurity Agencies

  1. Indian Computer Emergency Response Team (CERT-In)
    CERT-In is the national agency responsible for dealing with cybersecurity threats in India.
    CERT-In Alerts
  2. National Critical Information Infrastructure Protection Centre (NCIIPC)
    NCIIPC focuses on safeguarding India's critical information infrastructure, particularly in key sectors like energy, banking, telecom, and defense.
    NCIIPC Resources
  3. Cyber and Information Security Division (C&IS)
    Part of the Ministry of Home Affairs, C&IS is responsible for implementing cybersecurity policies.
    Ministry of Home Affairs - Cyber and Information Security
    Cybercrime Resources
  4. Defence Cyber Agency (DCA)
    The Defence Cyber Agency focuses on protecting the Indian Armed Forces' cyber infrastructure.
    Ministry of Defence
  5. National Cyber Coordination Centre (NCCC)
    NCCC operates under the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) for real-time threat assessment.
    Ministry of Electronics and IT - NCCC
  6. Data Security Council of India (DSCI)
    DSCI works in collaboration with the government to promote data protection and cybersecurity.
    DSCI Resources
